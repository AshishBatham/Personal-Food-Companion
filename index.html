Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the food calorie scanner?
Our AI technology has been trained on millions of food images and achieves over 90% accuracy for common foods. Accuracy may vary for complex dishes with multiple ingredients.
Is my data secure and private?
Yes, we take privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
Can I use the app without a subscription?
Absolutely! Our free version offers 5 body fat scans and 5 food scans per month, which is perfect for occasional users. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans and advanced features.
What devices are supported?
The app is available for both iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. It works on devices with iOS 12+ or Android 8+.
